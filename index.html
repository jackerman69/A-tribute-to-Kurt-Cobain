Kurt Cobain

A tribute to one of America's most talented musicians

Here is a time line of Kurt Cobain's life:

  • Born on February 20th, 1967 in Aberdeen, Washington.
  • He began singing at age two.
  • He was given his first guitar at age 14.
  • He was a very talented artist.
  • Some musical influences of Kurt were Led Zeppelin, The Sex Pistols, Velvet Underground, and The Beatles.
  • In 1987 formed the band Nirvana.
  • Released debut album Bleach in 1989.
  • Signed to DGC Records and released second album Nevermind in 1991.
  • Nirvana sold over 25 million albums in the U.S. and 75 million albums worldwide.
  • Bob Dylan cited "Polly" as Nirvana's best song.
  • On April 5th, 1994 Kurt Cobain took his own life at the age of 27.
  • Today there are many books and films about Kurt Cobain and the influence he had on the music industry
